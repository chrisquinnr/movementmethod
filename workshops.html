Workshops

We run 1 day workshops throughout the year at various locations in the UK.

The itinerary of each workshop will change during the year as we adapt and grow ourselves. We will cover topics such as the ones below but will adapt to cover what the attendees want on the day also. The day generally runs from 11am-4.30pm depending on location.

Facebook For specific details please check the Facebook Group

  • Handstand - the journey from basic kick ups to one arm control.
  • Gymnastic rings - breaking down and exploring mobility based exercises including German Hangs and strength based exercises such as Levers and the Muscle-up.
  • Movement - Working from isolation -> integration covering movements linked to many areas including crawling patterns, locomotion and dance.
  • Accessory/Flexibility/Structural Balance - The all important exercises and drills to ensure progression swiftly and safely.
  • Acrobatics - Having fun whilst gaining confidence!
